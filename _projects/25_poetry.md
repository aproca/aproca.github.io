One day you lay <br/>
high in bed, <br/>
realize it's been <br/>
six months already. <br/>
Still sleeping on the right <br/> 
side of the bed.

Time stopped half <br/>
a year I sit <br/>
still at the door. <br/>
Scarecrows staring  <br/>
across a growing dirt <br/>
road.
